Toys

A collection of small web apps.

  • Pickleball Scorekeeper
    Keep score for singles or doubles pickleball games with serve tracking and undo support.
  • What to Wear
    Travel clothing advisor that fetches real weather forecasts and suggests what to pack.
  • Global Counter
    A shared counter that persists across sessions — any visitor can increment it.
  • Silly Soundboard
    Tap colorful tiles to play synthesized silly sounds — horns, boings, farts, and more.
