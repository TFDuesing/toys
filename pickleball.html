Pickleball Rules Overview

Scoring

  • Only the serving team can score a point
  • Games are typically played to 11, win by 2
  • Tournament games may go to 15 or 21
  • Doubles score call: Server – Receiver – Server# (1 or 2)
  • Singles score call: Server – Receiver

Serving

  • Serve must be hit underhand, below the waist
  • Serve diagonally into the opposite service box
  • Both feet must be behind the baseline when serving
  • In doubles, each partner serves before a side-out (except the very first serve of the game)
  • A let serve (hits the net and lands in) is replayed

The Kitchen (NVZ)

  • The non-volley zone (NVZ) extends 7 ft from the net on each side
  • You cannot volley (hit the ball out of the air) while standing in the kitchen
  • You may enter the kitchen to play a ball that has bounced
  • You must exit the kitchen before volleying again

Double-Bounce Rule

  • After the serve, each side must let the ball bounce once before volleying
  • Once both sides have hit a ground stroke, volleys are allowed

Faults

  • Ball lands out of bounds
  • Ball hits the net
  • Volleying from the kitchen
  • Violating the double-bounce rule
