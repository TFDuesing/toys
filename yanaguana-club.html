About the Club

The Yanaguana Fiesta Club is a social club for San Antonio friends who love Fiesta season, local culture, and finding excuses to gather, eat, and celebrate. Named after the Payaya name for the sacred springs along the San Antonio River, we believe every gathering is worth celebrating in style.

We're just getting started — join us as we plan our first events, build our traditions, and carry on the spirit of one of America's oldest and most vibrant city festivals.
